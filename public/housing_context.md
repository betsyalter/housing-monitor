## Structural vs Cyclical Framework

**Cyclical factors (rate-driven, reversible):**
- Rate lock-in: ~70% of mortgages are below 4%, trapping sellers
- Affordability: monthly ownership cost vs rent spread at historic highs
- Fed constrained by oil/inflation from cutting rates

**Structural factors (demographic/supply, slower to reverse):**
- SFR REITs (INVH ~84k homes, AMH ~59k homes) absorbed existing supply and are not sellers
- Homeownership rate ceiling: younger cohorts shifting preference toward renting
- Second/third home owners turn over less frequently than primary

## Unlock Trigger Checklist
- [ ] 30yr mortgage rate drops below 5.5% (partial unlock)
- [ ] 30yr mortgage rate drops below 5.0% (significant unlock)
- [ ] Legislation: capital gains exclusion expansion, assumable mortgage reform
- [ ] Oil prices fall sustained below $70/bbl → Fed rate cut path opens
